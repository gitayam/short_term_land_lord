Twilio Setup Guide:
  1. Sign up for a Twilio account at twilio.com
  2. Navigate to Twilio Console
  3. Find your Account SID and Auth Token in the dashboard
  4. Add them to your .env file: 
    TWILIO_ACCOUNT_SID=ACxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx
TWILIO_AUTH_TOKEN=xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx
  5. Purchase a phone number from Phone Numbers → Buy a Number
  6. Enter the phone number here in E.164 format (e.g., +1234567890)
Note: Credentials must be in .env file for security. They cannot be edited here.
